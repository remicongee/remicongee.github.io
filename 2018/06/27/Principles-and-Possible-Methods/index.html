Target

Given a deep neural network $M$ and a dataset $D$ specific for certain tasks, the parameters $w$ of the network is determined by network structure and dataset. The general training target is seeking for parameters fitting well the very task, or more precisely, the posterior probability of $w$ knowing $M$ and $D$, denoted as $\mathbb{P}(w|M,D)$.
